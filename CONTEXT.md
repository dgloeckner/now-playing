# Now Playing

A full-screen iPad display that shows what's currently playing on Spotify, with album art and animated visuals. It does not play audio or control playback — the native Spotify app does that; this is a passive screen.

## Language

**Now Playing**:
The track currently playing on the active Spotify device, as rendered by the display (album art, title, artist, progress).
_Avoid_: Current song, active track

**Coming Up**:
The next track(s) in the Spotify playback queue, shown ahead of Now Playing.
_Avoid_: Up next, queue (the queue is the Spotify-side mechanism; "Coming Up" is what we render from it)

**Display Mode**:
A named visual treatment of the screen (layout, animation, effects). The display renders one Display Mode at a time.
_Avoid_: Theme, skin

**Party DJ Mode**:
A Display Mode emphasizing bold album art, motion, and club-style effects. Aesthetic only — it implies no interactive guest control.
_Avoid_: implying guests can queue or DJ
